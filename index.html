BIO

Katariina Tamm is an Estonian actor based in Tallinn and a graduate of the MA Acting programme at RADA. Her theatre work includes Theatre Ekspeditsioon, Von Krahl Theatre, and the Estonian Theatre for Young Audiences. In 2023, she was nominated for the Estonian Annual Theatre Awards. Languages: Estonian, English, Finnish, Russian
